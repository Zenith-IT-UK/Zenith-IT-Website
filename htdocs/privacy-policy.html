Privacy Policy

Date Created: January 20, 2025

Date Modified: N/A

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zenith IT (the "Company," "we," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://www.zenith-it.co.uk (the "Website"), interact with us, or use our services. By using our Website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when filling out forms on our Website (e.g., contact form), including:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (optional)
  • Any other information you choose to share with us via text field

Automatically Collected Data

When you access our Website, we automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and the time spent on them
  • Referring site details (e.g., the site that directed you to our Website)

Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your experience on our Website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Process your requests, respond to inquiries, and deliver consulting services.
  • Communication: Send you updates, service alerts, or marketing communications (where consent is required by law).
  • Website Improvement: Analyze how you use our Website to improve its design, functionality, and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with any applicable laws and regulations or respond to lawful requests by public authorities.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, email delivery), under obligations of confidentiality and security.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. International Transfers

Our primary operations are located in the United Kingdom. If you access our Website from outside the UK, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the UK. We will take reasonable measures to ensure your personal data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

7. Security Measures

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal data against loss, theft, and unauthorized use, access, or modification. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed secure, and we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us.

8. Your Rights (GDPR and UK Data Protection)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You may ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal limitations.
  • Objection: You may object to certain processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine- readable format.
